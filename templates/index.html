üß† Kociemba Algorithm (3x3 only)

The Kociemba algorithm is a two-phase method that guarantees solving any 3x3 cube in 20 moves or less:

  • Phase 1: Orient edges and position corners to reach "G1" subgroup
  • Phase 2: Solve the cube within G1 using only half-turns and U/D moves

Best for: Speed and optimality on 3x3 cubes

üîÑ Reduction Method (4x4, 5x5, NxN cubes)

The Reduction Method solves larger cubes by converting them into 3x3 equivalents:

  1. Solve Centers: Group center pieces of each face into solid blocks
  2. Pair Edges: Match edge pieces together to form "super edges"
  3. Solve as 3x3: Use standard 3x3 algorithms on the reduced cube

Why it works: After centers and edges are solved, the larger cube behaves exactly like a 3x3 cube, allowing us to use familiar solving techniques.

Best for: All cube sizes larger than 3x3

üìö Layer-by-Layer Method

A step-by-step beginner-friendly approach:

  1. Solve bottom cross (white cross)
  2. Complete first layer (white corners)
  3. Solve middle layer edges
  4. Form top cross (yellow cross)
  5. Orient top corners (all yellow on top)
  6. Position corners and edges correctly

Best for: Learning and understanding cube mechanics
